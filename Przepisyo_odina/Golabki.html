Recipe for golabki!

Golabki is a traditional polish food. It is made with white rice, cabbage, and meat and a simple souce on top.

Hears a recipe for the main dish:

You will need:

  • 1 white cabbage
  • 1 kg minced meat
  • 1 cup rice
  • Salt, pepper, marjoram to taste

And for the souce:

  • 500g toato puree
  • Salt, peeper and dried basil to taste

Steps:

  1. We cut out the core from the head of cabbage and put the cabbage in boiling salted water for about 5 minutes, removing the outer leaves, repeating the action several times. Then we get rid of the hard part of the leaves without damaging them.
  2. Cook the rice al dente. Mix the rice with the ground meat and spices. Put the meat on each leaf and roll it up.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices to the water in which the cabbage was boiled, cook for a while. Put cabbage leaves on the bottom of the pot, put cabbage rolls on them and pour tomato sauce over it
  4. Simmer for about 1.5-2 hours.
