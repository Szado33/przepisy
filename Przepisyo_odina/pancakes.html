Recipe for Pancakes!

Pancakes are a popular breakfast dish enjoyed worldwide.

Hears a recipe:

You will need:

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil, plus a little extra for frying
  • Something to put inside them

Steps:

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter. This should be similar in consistency to single cream.
  2. Set a medium frying pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper..
  3. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, using around half a ladleful of batter per pancake. Keep them warm in a low oven as you make the rest.
  4. Serve with your favourite filling.
